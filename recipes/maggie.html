Maggie

Recipe

Take 100 ml water in the bowl. Add maggie from the packet and put it on the stove for boil. once you realize maggie started cooking add masala packet inside and mix it. once all the water is vaporized your maggie is ready to serve.

Ingredients

  • maggie packet
  • Water
  • maggie masala

Steps

  1. Take water
  2. Add maggie
  3. Add maggie masala
  4. wait till water vapor
  5. maggie ready
