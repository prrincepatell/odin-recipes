Tea

Recipe

First take water in the tapeli. Add 2 spoon tea and sugar in the water. after that add ginger. let the tea boil for some time. once tea is boiled and you can see tea colour add milk. let the milk properly boil for some time. after 2-3 ukala your tea is ready to serve.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea
  • sugar
  • Milk
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Add water
  2. Add tea and sugar
  3. Add ginger
  4. Add milk
